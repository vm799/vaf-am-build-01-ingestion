OUTLOOK

The Group expects continued benefit from:
  • Higher interest rates (estimated +15-20bps NIM for each 25bps rise)
  • Cost productivity initiatives (£2.5bn annual target by 2027)
  • Wealth management organic growth (target 8-10% annually)
  • Market share gains in commercial banking

Near-term headwinds:
  • Potential credit normalization if recession emerges
  • Geopolitical uncertainty affecting M&A pipeline
  • Regulatory capital requirement increases (estimated +50bps by 2027)
